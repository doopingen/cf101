About

The City Snooze Story began with our fearless leader and founder Kerry P. Berton. Kerry is a lifelong narcoleptic, who over a lifelong career of run-ins with police, made it her mission to challenge societal constructs on what is and isnt a proper sleeping space. Kerry, and all of us at City Snooze, know that sleep is the most important third of your day, and in our fast past world more than 37% of Americans aren't getting their doctor recommended sleep. Through the advent of Snooze Cubes in major cities, we are making an impact by providing a safe, affordable place for Americans of all kinds to catch some Z's.

City Snooze offers sound proof capsule units to Seattleites seeking a quiet space to rest, relex and recharge. Your snooze pod comes fully equipped with a television, wifi internet connection, hot towel, and lights.

Whether you are seeking a short nap, or a more permanent rest, City Snooze is good for you. With a local, refreshing sleep space, you will awaken rejuvenated and prepared to greet the world with fresh eyes.
